Description

Siamés Studio is an independent video game studio based in Madrid, Spain. The team is composed of students who are currently studying video game development at Rey Juan Carlos University.

History

In October, 2020 we were prompted to make groups for certain university projects. And that is how Siamés Studio was born. We were reunited by our love for video games and our love for cats.

Projects

    Gestionas un restaurante cafetería y preaparas platos de ramen, cafés y tortitas a tus comensales gatunos. Tiene un estilo pixel art amistoso y colorido.
